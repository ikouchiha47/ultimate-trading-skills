## How to read a PSU bank for swing/position (the method)
1. **Structure first** (price vs 50 & 200-DMA): above both = uptrend (buy dips to 50-DMA); below
   200-DMA = downtrend (stand aside until reclaimed). This is the EARNED edge's home.
2. **Confirm with flow**: rising **delivery%** (investors, not traders) + **absorption** (buyer
   soaking supply) on a dip = real demand; low delivery + low absorption on weakness = no bid.
3. **Profits**: is earnings momentum supporting price, or is a high TTM number just a low-base
   artefact (the IOB/UCOBANK trap)?
4. **Valuation**: P/B vs ROE — pay up only where ROE justifies it; cheap + improving = best risk/reward.
5. **Investment/loan book**: where is growth coming from (RBI: Services/NBFC + retail/housing lead).

The PSU bank cohort re-rated hard through the asset-quality cleanup; the cohort is now **dispersing**
— winners (MAHABANK/INDIANB/UNIONBANK/CANBK) pulling above their 200-DMAs on real earnings, laggards

cheapness is a falling knife** (and whose TTM "growth" is a low-base illusion). Growth markets per
RBI: Services/NBFC (+27.7%), retail/housing — favour banks leaning into those.
